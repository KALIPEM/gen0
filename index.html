What is
prompt engineering ?

Prompt Engineering, in this aspect, is the systematic utilization of tools to efficiently achieve tasks or goals, optimizing workflows for maximum effectiveness. When it comes to AI and ML, Prompt Engineering is the process of designing and refining prompts or stimuli in the context of natural language processing (NLP) tasks.
